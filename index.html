ABOUT US

SOLUTION-DRIVEN

Since our inception in 2000, our objective to realise excellence has remained steadfast and clear. We introduce the newest technologies to our manufacturing process.

SERVICE-DRIVEN

Supported by a strong foundation merging modern expertise with engaged partnerships, OBP is committed to making the difference in key areas affecting our local and global communities.

TECHNOLOGY-DRIVEN

We have evolved through a rich past defined by world firsts. Today, OBP embraces technology enhanced by digital solutions that answer modern animal health challenges.


SALES & DISTRIBUTION


YOUR FIRST CHOICE IN ANIMAL HEALTH SOLUTIONS

Our consultants demonstrate their expertise by providing comprehensive product advice and guidance for our customers. Our mission is to source and supply cost-effective animal health solutions for our customers.

SALES

We continuously enhance our product portfolio to serve our customer needs. We endeavour to make continuous improvements to vaccine efficacy, safety, and ease of administration for the wellbeing of animal herds.

DISTRIBUTION

As an entity, we believe access to veterinary vaccines should be universal. We maintain strong partnerships with service providers within our supply channels to ensure access to our products locally and internationally.

TRAINING

OBP continues in our efforts to train both new and emerging farmers through market reach. We maintain existing partnerships and facilitate access markets for the benefit of small producers.

EDUCATION

We support the empowerment of youth, women, and people living with disabilities, as well as black-owned businesses. Our aim is to make a positive impact on animal health through sustained programs and educational initiatives.

The Procurement Process at OBP


OUR CURRENT PROCUREMENT NEEDS

OBP regularly updates our requirements for consideration to all potential bidders. You will find the specifications for each service or product in a detailed, downloadable format. OBP retains the right to award or to forgo awarding any specified scope of work. Site inspections may occur, subject to the nature of the work required. All prospective bidders will be notified, accordingly.

IDENTIFY YOUR BUSINESS SUITABILITY

Qualify your business profile, financial capabilities and ability to deliver against all requirements listed on your RFQ of choice. We suggest that potential bidders contact us should they need further clarification.

CREATE A PROFILE ON OUR PORTAL

In accordance with our mandate to realise a green footprint, and through our partnership with ProAgrica, we require submission of all relevant documentation to be made via our online portal. Here, you will be able to register a profile, and fill in each field as prompted.

PROVIDE US WITH RELEVANT INFORMATION

We encourage all potential partners to provide as much detail as possible in their RFQ submissions. Please make sure that you have quantified and qualified each facet within your response. We look forward to receiving your proposal.
